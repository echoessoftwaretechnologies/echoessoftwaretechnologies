Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our implementation process, pricing, and technical requirements.

What is your typical implementation timeline?

Implementation timelines vary based on project complexity. Simple applications typically take 8-12 weeks, while enterprise solutions may require 6-12 months. We provide detailed project timelines during our initial consultation phase.

How do you handle pricing for custom development projects?

We offer flexible pricing models including fixed-price projects, time and materials, and dedicated team arrangements. Pricing is determined after a thorough requirements analysis and is always transparent with no hidden costs.

What level of support do you provide post-implementation?

We offer comprehensive 24/7 support packages including monitoring, maintenance, updates, and technical assistance. Our support tiers range from basic maintenance to full managed services depending on your needs.

What are the minimum technical requirements for your solutions?

Technical requirements vary by solution. We support cloud-native deployments on AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud, as well as on-premises installations. Our team conducts a technical assessment to ensure compatibility with your existing infrastructure.

Do you provide training for our internal teams?

Yes, we include comprehensive training as part of our implementation process. This covers end-user training, administrator training, and technical documentation. We also offer ongoing training sessions as your team grows.

How do you ensure data security and compliance?

Security is built into every solution we develop. We follow industry best practices, maintain SOC 2 compliance, and can meet specific regulatory requirements like GDPR, HIPAA, or SOX depending on your industry needs.

Can you integrate with our existing systems?

Absolutely. We specialize in system integration and can connect new solutions with your existing ERP, CRM, databases, and third-party applications through APIs and custom integration layers.

What happens if we need changes after the project is completed?

We build solutions with scalability and flexibility in mind. Post-implementation changes can be handled through our maintenance agreements or separate enhancement projects. We maintain detailed documentation to facilitate future modifications.
